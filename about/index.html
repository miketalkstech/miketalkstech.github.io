About Me

Hello! I'm Michael (Mike) Bartolomeo.

I've been a Librarian and Technology Instructor since 2016, helping people of all ages get more comfortable with technology.

I design and teach digital literacy programs on topics like artificial intelligence, online privacy, and essential tech skills. I also speak at library conferences and staff development events.

Most of my work is with public libraries and community organizations, but I also work with businesses and schools. I teach in person and virtually.
